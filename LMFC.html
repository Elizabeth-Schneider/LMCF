Camp
Needlepoint
Camp Needlepoint - The purpose is to provide a fun and safe camping experience for children living with diabetes. We want to give kids the opportunity to meet other kids just like them as well as help them gain confidence and independence in managing their diabetes.

Camp is located at YMCA Camp St. Croix, south of Hudson, WI on a wooded bluff along the St. Croix River. Facilities include: a 24 hour health center, dining hall, crafts building, nature building, tennis and basketball courts, soccer fields, archery range, sand volleyball court, rope course and horseback riding area.
